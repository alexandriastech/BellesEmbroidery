Belle's Embroidery

Welcome to Belle's Embroidery. This is a website for a pseudo-business that sells embroidery materials and hosts an embroidery club meeting every Wednesday. This website was created by Alexandria. Above, there are four links: Our Story, Getting Started, Our Club, and FAQs. Please feel free to click one of the links to explore more!

Materials

These are the materials you may need to get started!


An embroidery hoop and threaded cloth will be needed. This is where your design will be sewn; you can choose whatever cloth you would like but it works best on cloth meant for embroidery.

You will needles and somewhere to place them as well such as a pincushion.

This is the most exciting part! You can pick as many threads as you would like; you can also have as many colors as you want too!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are our most frequently asked questions:

  • Where can I buy threads?

    • Look at Amazon and Etsy.

  • Where can I learn how to thread?

    • Places such as Youtube and Udemy.

  • How long does it take to be good at embroidery?

    • Take your time, and your work will even shine! Fun first!
